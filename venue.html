Venue & Travel Information

Discover beautiful Naxos, Cyclades, Greece

About Naxos

Naxos is the largest and most fertile island in the Cyclades, combining stunning beaches, ancient history, and traditional Greek island charm. The island offers an ideal setting for academic study with its peaceful atmosphere, rich cultural heritage, and excellent infrastructure.

Climate

July weather: Sunny and warm with temperatures averaging 25-30°C (77-86°F). Perfect Mediterranean summer climate with cool evening breezes.

Culture

Rich archaeological sites including Temple of Apollo, ancient marble quarries, and Byzantine churches. Traditional villages and authentic Greek cuisine.

Accessibility

Well-connected to Athens by ferry (3.5-5 hours) and direct flights (45 minutes). Easy walking town center and reliable public transportation.

Getting to Naxos

Option 1: Ferry from Athens (Recommended)

From: Piraeus Port, Athens

Duration: 3.5 to 5 hours depending on ferry type

Cost: Approximately €40-60

Booking: Ferry tickets at FerryHopper.com or GreekFerries.gr

Tip: Book in advance during summer. High-speed ferries are faster but more expensive.

Option 2: Flight to Naxos

From: Athens International Airport (ATH)

Duration: 45 minutes

Cost: €80-150 depending on season

Airlines: Olympic Air, Sky Express

Tip: Flights can be limited. Book early for best prices and availability.

Accommodation

Participants arrange their own accommodation. We recommend staying in Naxos Town (Chora) for easy access to classes and amenities.

Recommended Areas

Naxos Town (Chora)

Best for: Convenience and nightlife

Walking distance to everything. Numerous restaurants, cafes, and shops. Old Town charm.

Budget: €50-80/night

Agios Georgios Beach

Best for: Beach access

10-minute walk to town center. Right on the beach. Quieter atmosphere.

Budget: €60-100/night

Agios Prokopios

Best for: Beautiful beaches

Bus connection to town. Stunning sandy beaches. More relaxed setting.

Budget: €70-120/night

Booking Platforms

Local Transportation

  • On Foot: Naxos Town is very walkable
  • Local Bus: KTEL buses connect major beaches and villages (€1.80-3 per trip)
  • Taxi: Available but limited. Agree on price before starting
  • Car/Scooter Rental: Many rental shops in town. International license required.

  • Power adapter (European plug type C/F)
  • Travel insurance documentation
  • Copies of important documents
